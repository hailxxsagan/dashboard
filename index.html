Amy Haukka

Amy Haukka (@epiphamy)

JOYCE MANOR

I saw Joyce Manor in January at the Palladium in Los Angeles. They played to their biggest crowd yet! It was such an honor to be there and the energy was unreal! If I could only see one band live forever, I'd choose them.

WAVVES

I saw Wavves twice in one night! What? They played at the Soda Bar in November. We went to the first show, and had so much fun that we just had to get tickets for the next one. What a blast!

IDLES

I saw IDLES for the first time in April at SOMA! They played the mainstage. Apparently there was an earthquake during the set, but I think that was just IDLES rocking the house!

SKEGSS

I saw Skegss in April at the Music Box! It was a pretty short set, but the crowd loved every song! Glad they came to SD between Coachella sets. Oh, and I lost my shoe... oops.

TOGETHER PANGEA

I saw Together Pangea at SOMA in September. It was such a blast, and the crowd was all ages!

OK SHORE

I saw Ok Shore in July! There's nothing like seeing your friends perform their own music live - what a treat!
